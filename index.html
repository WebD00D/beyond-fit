OUR MISSION
PROVIDE AND IMPLEMENT WELL DESIGNED, COLLABORATIVE PROGRAMS FOR CLIENTS IN AND OUT OF THE GYM.
The mission of Beyond Fit is to provide and implement well designed, collaborative programs for clients in and out of the gym. Our goal is to provide them with the knowledge, motivation, and training to enhance their physical and physiological activity. We believe Beyond Fit is the environment where successful goals are met through the implementation of scientific knowledge to develop structured, proper exercise selection. Clients will train systematically in a safe and professional environment with emphasis on injury prevention and high physical demands.
OUR TEAM
David Kim
Received first bachelor’s in kinesiology and health sciences, with a concentration in exercise science, and a second bachelor’s in psychology, both from the Virginia Commonwealth University. Certified strength and conditioning specialist (CSCS) from the NSCA. Excels in a wide variety of experiences with different populations regarding strength, power, muscular endurance, hypertrophy, and weight loss. Focuses on the utilization of weight bearing exercises along with free-weight, ground based, and multi joint movements. Baylor Conde
Nationally certified Personal Trainer, as well as a Former Collegiate and multi-sport athlete in High School. As an athlete he’s developed a great understanding of the discipline it takes to excel in fitness and lifestyle changes. Found his passion for fitness after undergoing a complete lifestyle change in High School. He went from being 270lbs to a healthy athletic 189lbs. As motivated as he is about his own fitness, he is most passionate about helping others reach their fitness potential and achieving success in their own lifestyle transformations. He values the process involved with fitness and the journey individuals undergo to create a better self. Baylor trained under many coaches in different sports and been in the gym/fitness industry most his life, which led to his pursuit of starting his own Brand and opening his own gym. He’s taken everything he’s learned through the years and with continued educational advancement applies it to his clients to ensure they safely and actively achieve their goals.
